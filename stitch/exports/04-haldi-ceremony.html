The Ceremony

The Haldi ceremony is a lively pre-wedding ritual where a paste of turmeric, sandalwood, and rose water is applied to the bride and groom. Traditionally, it's meant to ward off evil spirits and provide a natural glow for the wedding day.

We invite you to wear your brightest shades of yellow or orange and join us for a morning of folk songs, laughter, and perhaps a little bit of playful turmeric-smearing!
